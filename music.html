Muuusic

A personal project with flow, neon & swag

Every trimester, our boss challenge us to make something amazing. Something we'd proud of. I was thinking working on some project makes me happy. Something everybody uses everyday, every moment. Something I can't live without it. Obviously: My Muuusic. With x3U because I really live every note, every song, every second of every piece.

The main difference between Muuusic and the rest of music players is that you can play with the sound wave to move through the different movements and intensities of each song.
